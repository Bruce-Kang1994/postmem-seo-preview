Three Different Paths to Preserving Family Stories

保存家庭故事的三条不同路径

There is no single "best" family story app. There are three fundamentally different approaches — and the right choice depends on how your family communicates.

不存在"最好的"家庭故事应用。这里有三种本质不同的方式——正确的选择取决于你的家人如何沟通。

  • StoryWorth asks someone to write.
  • StoryWorth 让人写作。
  • Remento asks someone to speak.
  • Remento 让人口述。
  • PostMem starts with the photos you already have.
  • PostMem 从你已有的照片出发。

Each approach works well for the right family and falls flat for the wrong one. The question isn't which tool has the most features — it's which one your family will actually finish.

每种方式对于合适的家庭都很出色，对于不合适的家庭则悄然失败。真正的问题不是哪个工具功能最多——而是哪个你的家人能真正完成。

We built PostMem, so we have a bias. We'll be transparent about it throughout. We also genuinely believe StoryWorth and Remento are good products for the right families.

PostMem 是我们做的产品，所以我们有偏见——我们会在全文中坦诚面对。同时，我们也真心认为 StoryWorth 和 Remento 对于合适的家庭来说是好产品。

StoryWorth: The Writing Path

StoryWorth：写作之路

How It Works

使用方式

StoryWorth sends one question per week via email — "What's your earliest memory?" or "Tell me about your first job." The recipient writes their answer, and after 52 weeks, all the answers are compiled into a hardcover book. The concept is simple and proven — StoryWorth has been around for over a decade.

StoryWorth 每周通过邮件发送一个问题。收件人写下回答，52 周后所有答案被编排成一本精装书。概念简单且经过验证——StoryWorth 已经存在十多年了。

Best For

最适合

Families where the storyteller enjoys writing. Your mom journals every day. Your dad is a retired professor. Your grandmother has been saying "I should write my memoirs" for years.

讲故事的人喜欢写作的家庭。你妈妈每天写日记，你爸爸是退休教授，你奶奶说了好几年"我应该写回忆录"。

Strengths

优势

  • +Beautiful printed books that feel like genuine family heirlooms
  • 精美的印刷书，犹如真正的家族传家宝
  • +Over a decade in the market — established, trusted, well-reviewed
  • 十余年市场经验——成熟、可信赖、口碑好
  • +Simple concept with no learning curve — it's just email
  • 概念简单，零学习成本——就是邮件

Tradeoffs

不足

  • -Writing fatigue — Reddit threads describe mid-year dropout with rich early chapters and thin later ones
  • 写作疲劳——Reddit 帖子描述了年中放弃的现象，前面章节丰富后面却很薄
  • -Starts from a blank page with no visual memory triggers
  • 从空白页开始，没有视觉记忆触发
  • -One-year commitment — incomplete year produces disconnected fragments
  • 需要一整年的坚持——中途放弃只会留下零散片段

Pricing

定价

$99/year, includes one printed hardcover book. Additional copies available for purchase.

$99/年，含一本精装印刷书。额外副本可另购。

Output

成品

A printed hardcover book mailed to you, plus digital access to all written stories.

一本寄到你手上的精装印刷书，以及所有书面故事的数字访问权限。

Remento: The Speaking Path

Remento：口述之路

How It Works

使用方式

Remento provides guided prompts, but instead of writing, the storyteller records spoken answers — on video or audio. Remento's AI transcribes the recordings and helps edit them into written narratives. The final product is a printed book with QR codes that link to the original audio recordings.

Remento 提供引导式提示，但讲述者不用写，而是录制口述回答——视频或音频。Remento 的 AI 转录录音并帮助编辑成书面叙事。最终成品是一本带二维码的印刷书，扫码即可听到原始音频。

Best For

最适合

Families where the storyteller comes alive in conversation. Your dad holds the room at Thanksgiving dinner but would never type a paragraph. Your grandmother's stories are best when you hear the pauses, the laughter, the way she says certain words.

讲故事的人在对话中才最鲜活的家庭。你爸爸在感恩节晚餐上是全场焦点，但让他打一段字绝无可能。你奶奶的故事在你听到那些停顿、笑声和她说某些词的方式时最动人。

Strengths

优势

  • +Speaking is easier than writing for most older adults
  • 对大多数老年人来说，说比写容易得多
  • +Voice preservation through QR codes is genuinely unique
  • 通过二维码保存声音，是真正独特的功能
  • +Guided but flexible — follow the structure or go off-script
  • 有引导但灵活——可以按结构走，也可以自由发挥

Tradeoffs

不足

  • -Still requires scheduling recording sessions
  • 仍然需要安排录制时间
  • -Not everyone likes being recorded — can feel performative
  • 有些人不喜欢被录音——可能感觉很刻意
  • -Audio quality varies with equipment and environment
  • 音频质量受设备和环境影响较大

Pricing

定价

Approximately $99/year.

约 $99/年。

Output

成品

A printed book with QR codes linking to original audio/video recordings, plus digital access.

一本带二维码的印刷书，扫码可听原始音频/视频录制，以及数字访问权限。

PostMem: The Photo Path

PostMem：照片之路

How It Works

使用方式

PostMem takes a different starting point: your existing family photos. Upload photos from your phone, iCloud, Google Photos, or an old hard drive. PostMem's AI identifies faces, recognizes places, reads timestamps, and groups related photos into meaningful clusters. For each photo group, AI asks you targeted questions. You answer with a voice note or a few typed words. AI organizes your answers into a written story attached to the photos.

PostMem 以不同的起点出发：你已有的家庭照片。从手机、iCloud、Google Photos 或老硬盘上传照片。PostMem 的 AI 识别人脸、辨认地点、读取时间戳，并将相关照片归为有意义的组合。对于每组照片，AI 向你提出针对性问题。你用语音或几个字回答，AI 将回答整理成附在照片上的故事。

The core idea: AI surfaces the context; you add the meaning.

核心理念：AI 挖掘线索，你赋予意义。

Best For

最适合

Families with large photo libraries and no desire for a year-long writing project. You have 40,000 photos on your phone and three boxes of albums in the closet. Nobody wants weekly homework.

照片很多但不想做一整年写作项目的家庭。你手机里有 40,000 张照片，衣柜里还有三箱相册。没人想做每周的"作业"。

Strengths

优势

  • +Starts with what you already have — thousands of existing photos
  • 从你已有的东西出发——成千上万张现有照片
  • +No weekly commitment — add stories when inspiration strikes
  • 无需每周打卡——灵感来了随时添加故事
  • +AI finds connections across decades you'd never discover manually
  • AI 能发现跨越数十年的关联，你手动永远不会注意到

Tradeoffs

不足

  • -Newer product — doesn't have StoryWorth's decade of market presence
  • 较新的产品——没有 StoryWorth 十年的市场积淀
  • -Requires digital photos (physical albums need scanning first)
  • 需要数字照片（实体相册需要先扫描）
  • -Primary output is digital; printed book is optional, not the default
  • 主要输出是数字化的；印刷书是可选项，不是默认体验

Pricing

定价

Starting at $19/month ($228/year). Printed books are a separate cost.

$19/月起（$228/年）。印刷书费用另计。

Output

成品

Digital stories with shareable links, organized around your photo library. Optional print export.

数字故事 + 分享链接，围绕你的照片库组织。可选印刷导出。

When PostMem Is Not the Right Choice

PostMem 不适合的情况

We built PostMem, so we think it's great — but it's not for everyone. Consider a different tool if:

PostMem 是我们做的，我们当然觉得它好——但它不适合所有人。在以下情况下考虑其他工具：

Your family has no digital photos and doesn't want to digitize. If everything lives in physical albums and nobody wants to scan, PostMem's photo-first approach won't help. Start with StoryWorth or Remento instead.

你的家庭没有数字照片也不想数字化。如果所有照片都在实体相册里且没人想扫描，PostMem 的照片优先方式帮不上忙。考虑 StoryWorth 或 Remento。

The storyteller prefers structured weekly prompts. Some people thrive with a recurring writing ritual. If that's your family, StoryWorth's 52-week cadence is a feature, not a bug.

讲述者喜欢结构化的每周提示。有些人在重复的写作仪式中表现最好。如果那是你的家人，StoryWorth 的 52 周节奏是优点，不是缺点。

You specifically want to preserve someone's speaking voice. PostMem captures stories through text and photos, not audio. If hearing Grandma's voice 30 years from now matters most, Remento's QR-to-audio feature is purpose-built for this.

你特别想保存某人的声音。PostMem 通过文字和照片捕捉故事，不是音频。如果 30 年后听到奶奶的声音最重要，Remento 的 QR 音频功能就是为此而生的。

You want a finished printed book as the primary deliverable. StoryWorth's entire workflow leads to a hardcover book. PostMem is digital-first with optional print.

你想要一本完成的印刷书作为主要成品。StoryWorth 的整个流程通向一本精装书。PostMem 是数字优先，印刷是可选项。

You want a fully mature, established platform. StoryWorth has been operating for over 10 years. PostMem is newer. If track record and brand recognition matter to you, StoryWorth has the edge.

你想要一个完全成熟的平台。StoryWorth 运营超过 10 年。PostMem 更新。如果你看重历史记录和品牌知名度，StoryWorth 占优。

What Real Users Say: Three Family Scenarios

真实用户怎么说：三个家庭场景

These scenarios are composites based on common patterns from our user research and public reviews — not quotes from specific individuals.

以下场景基于用户研究和公开评论中的常见模式组合——不是特定个人的引述。

"My mom is a retired teacher who journals every day."

"我妈妈是退休教师，每天都写日记。"

Best fit: StoryWorth. She already loves writing. The weekly prompt gives her a reason to write about specific memories. The printed book will become a family treasure. Don't fix what isn't broken — if your family has a writer, let them write.

最佳选择：StoryWorth。她本身就热爱写作。每周的提示给她一个理由去写特定的回忆。印刷出来的书将成为家庭珍藏。

"My dad would talk your ear off at dinner, but he'd never type a paragraph."

"我爸在饭桌上能说得你耳朵起茧，但让他打一段字绝无可能。"

Best fit: Remento. His stories come alive when he talks. Recording him captures his personality — the pauses, the laugh. The QR audio feature means future generations get the real him, not just his words on paper.

最佳选择：Remento。他一说起来故事就活了。录制他能捕捉个性——停顿、笑声。QR 音频功能意味着后代能听到真实的他。

"We have 47,000 photos across three phones, two laptops, and an old hard drive. Nobody wants homework."

"我们三部手机、两台笔记本和一个老硬盘里有 47,000 张照片。没人想做'作业'。"

Best fit: PostMem. The photos already exist. The stories are trapped inside them. PostMem's AI identifies the people and moments, asks targeted questions, and organizes the answers — no weekly commitment, no blank page.

最佳选择：PostMem。照片已经在那里了。故事被困在照片里。PostMem 的 AI 识别人物和时刻，提出针对性问题，整理答案——无需每周打卡，无需面对空白页。

Can You Combine Approaches?

可以组合使用吗？

Yes, and some families get the best results this way. The tools aren't mutually exclusive.

可以，而且有些家庭这样做效果最好。这些工具不是互斥的。

A concrete example: Start with PostMem to organize a large photo library. As PostMem groups photos and surfaces forgotten moments, you'll notice stories that deserve deeper treatment. Use Remento to record an in-depth voice interview about those specific moments, with the photos open as prompts. Now you have organized digital stories with photos (PostMem) plus the storyteller's voice telling the most important ones (Remento). Two tools, one complete archive.

一个具体例子：先用 PostMem 整理大量照片库。当 PostMem 将照片分组并浮现被遗忘的时刻时，你会注意到一些值得深入记录的故事。用 Remento 针对那些特定时刻录制深入的语音访谈，把照片作为提示打开。现在你有了带照片的数字故事（PostMem）加上讲述者讲述最重要故事的声音（Remento）。两个工具，一份完整的档案。

The Verdict: Match the Tool to Your Family

结论：为你的家庭匹配合适的工具

There is no universal winner. The best family story preservation tool is the one your family will actually complete.

没有通用赢家。最好的家庭故事保存工具，是你的家人会真正完成使用的那一款。

  • • Choose StoryWorth if your family has a confident writer who enjoys the weekly ritual. ($99/year)
  • 如果你的家人中有一位自信的写作者，享受每周的写作仪式，选择 StoryWorth。（$99/年）
  • • Choose Remento if your family's stories come alive through voice and conversation. (~$99/year)
  • 如果你家的故事在口述和对话中才最鲜活，选择 Remento。（约 $99/年）
  • • Choose PostMem if your family has thousands of photos and nobody wants a year-long writing project. ($19/month)
  • 如果你家有成千上万张照片，没人想花一整年写东西，选择 PostMem。（$19/月）

The most important thing? Just start. Pick the tool that feels right, try it with one story, and see what happens. Your family's stories are worth preserving — in whatever format works for you.

最重要的事？就是开始。选一款感觉对的工具，试着记录一个故事，看看会发生什么。你家人的故事值得保存——用任何对你们有效的格式。

Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

Which of the three is cheapest? ▾

三个里面哪个最便宜？

StoryWorth is the most affordable on an annual basis at $99/year (about $8.25/month), and that includes one printed hardcover book. Remento is also approximately $99/year. PostMem starts at $19/month, which comes to $228/year — the lowest monthly entry point, but the highest annual cost of the three. If annual budget is your primary concern, StoryWorth is the cheapest option. PostMem and Remento charge separately for printed books.

按年计算，StoryWorth 最实惠，$99/年（约 $8.25/月），且包含一本精装印刷书。Remento 也约为 $99/年。PostMem 起价 $19/月（$228/年）——月度起步价最低，但年度总费用在三者中最高。如果年度预算是你主要的考虑因素，StoryWorth 最便宜。PostMem 和 Remento 的印刷书需另付费。

Which tool has the highest completion rate? ▾

哪个工具的完成率最高？

No independent study compares completion rates across these three tools. We can say this: the approach matters. Photo-based tools require less ongoing effort because the photos already exist. Writing-based tools require consistent weekly commitment over a full year, which many families struggle to maintain based on user reviews. Voice-based tools fall in between. We don't have hard numbers, and anyone claiming specific completion rates without citing a source is guessing.

目前没有独立研究直接对比完成率。但方式很重要：照片类工具需要的持续投入更少，因为照片已经存在。写作类工具需要全年每周坚持，根据用户评价很多家庭难以维持。语音类工具介于两者之间。我们没有确切数据，任何声称有具体完成率但不引用来源的说法都是猜测。

Can I use more than one of these tools? ▾

我可以同时使用多个工具吗？

Yes. See the "Can You Combine Approaches?" section above for a concrete example. Start with one, and add another if you need a different format for different family members.

可以。请参阅上面"可以组合使用吗？"部分的具体例子。从一个开始，如果不同家人需要不同格式再添加另一个。

Can I switch between tools later? ▾

以后可以切换工具吗？

Yes, but content transfer isn't seamless between platforms. StoryWorth stories can be exported as text. Remento recordings are yours to keep. PostMem stories can be exported as text and images. If you're unsure, start with a free trial where available before committing.

可以，但平台之间的内容迁移并不无缝。StoryWorth 的故事可以导出为文字。Remento 的录音归你所有。PostMem 的故事可以导出为文字和图片。如果不确定，先试用免费版本。

What if my family member isn't tech-savvy? ▾

如果家人不太懂技术怎么办？

All three tools require basic smartphone or computer skills. StoryWorth is email-based, which most people are comfortable with. Remento requires recording audio or video. PostMem requires uploading photos. For someone truly uncomfortable with technology, consider a low-tech approach first: sit with them, open a photo album together, record the conversation on your phone, and transcribe later. You can always upload the results to any of these tools afterward.

三个工具都需要基本的智能手机或电脑操作能力。StoryWorth 基于邮件，大多数人都很熟悉。Remento 需要录音/录像。PostMem 需要上传照片。对于真的不擅长科技的家人，先考虑低技术方案：坐在他们身边，一起翻开相册，用手机录下对话，之后再转录。你随时可以把内容上传到任何工具里。
