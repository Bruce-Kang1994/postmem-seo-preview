Turn Family Photos Into Stories With AI — Here's How PostMem Works

用 AI 把家庭照片变成故事——PostMem 的工作原理

You have thousands of family photos spread across your phone, an old laptop, iCloud, maybe a box in the closet. Each one holds a memory. But right now, they're just files — unnamed, unsorted, and slowly losing context as the people who remember them get older.

你有成千上万张家庭照片散布在手机、旧笔记本电脑、iCloud，也许还有衣柜里的一个箱子。每张都承载着一段记忆。但现在，它们只是文件——没有名字、没有分类，随着记得它们的人越来越老，背景信息在慢慢流失。

PostMem turns those scattered photos into organized, written family stories. You upload your photos. AI groups them by people, places, and moments. Then it asks you questions about what it finds — and organizes your answers into stories you can share with family or print as a book.

PostMem 把这些散落的照片变成有条理的家庭文字故事。你上传照片。AI 按人物、地点和时刻分组。然后它就发现的内容向你提问——并把你的回答整理成可以与家人分享或印刷成书的故事。

One thing to know upfront: PostMem does not write your family's stories for you. AI surfaces what's in your photos and asks the right questions. You provide the meaning. The stories are yours — your memories, your words, your voice.

有一点要事先说明：PostMem 不会替你写家庭故事。AI 挖掘照片中的内容并提出正确的问题。你提供意义。故事是你的——你的记忆、你的话语、你的声音。

What Is Photo-to-Story AI?

什么是照片转故事 AI？

"Photo-to-story AI" is a new category. Most people haven't heard the term because, until recently, the technology to make it work didn't exist. Here's what it means in practice:

"照片转故事 AI"是一个新品类。大多数人没听过这个词，因为直到最近，使其成为可能的技术才出现。以下是它在实际中的含义：

Photo-to-story AI reads the visual content in your photos — faces, locations, timestamps, objects, text — and uses that information to group related photos together. It then asks you specific questions about each group. You answer. AI organizes your answers into a written narrative attached to those photos.

照片转故事 AI 读取你照片中的视觉内容——人脸、地点、时间戳、物体、文字——然后用这些信息把相关照片归组。接着它向你提出关于每组的具体问题。你回答。AI 把你的回答整理成与这些照片关联的书面叙述。

To be explicit about what AI does and doesn't do here:

明确说明 AI 在这里做什么和不做什么：

  • ✓AI identifies faces, locations, dates, and visual patterns across your photos
  • AI 识别你照片中的人脸、地点、日期和视觉模式
  • ✓AI groups related photos into moments and timelines
  • AI 分组相关照片为时刻和时间线
  • ✓AI asks you targeted questions about what it finds
  • AI 提问关于它发现的内容的针对性问题
  • ✓AI organizes your spoken or typed answers into readable narratives
  • AI 整理你的口述或文字回答为可读的叙述
  • ✗AI does not invent facts, guess relationships, or fill in details you didn't provide
  • AI 不会编造事实、猜测关系或填充你没有提供的细节

The stories come from you. AI is the structure; you are the source.

故事来自你。AI 是结构；你是来源。

How PostMem Works (3 Steps)

PostMem 如何工作（3 个步骤）

1

Upload Your Photos

上传你的照片

What you do: Connect your iCloud or Google Photos account, or upload directly from your phone, computer, or external hard drive. Any common image format works (JPG, PNG, HEIC, TIFF).

你做什么：连接 iCloud 或 Google Photos 账户，或从手机、电脑或外置硬盘直接上传。支持常见图片格式（JPG、PNG、HEIC、TIFF）。

What AI does: Scans every photo for faces, reads timestamps and location data, identifies visual patterns, and groups related photos into clusters. Duplicates and near-duplicates are flagged.

AI 做什么：扫描每张照片的人脸，读取时间戳和位置数据，识别视觉模式，并将相关照片聚类。标记重复和近似重复。

Realistic example: You upload 847 photos from your phone's camera roll. PostMem groups them into 34 moments. Some groups are spot-on ("Thanksgiving 2019 — 11 photos"). Others need your help ("3 people at a table — location unknown"). The whole process takes about 4 minutes for 847 photos.

真实示例：你从手机相册上传了 847 张照片。PostMem 将它们分成 34 个时刻。有些分组很准确（"2019 年感恩节——11 张照片"）。有些需要你帮助（"3 个人在桌旁——地点未知"）。847 张照片的处理大约需要 4 分钟。

产品截图：上传后的照片库视图——展示 4-5 个自动生成的照片分组，带缩略图预览、建议标签和照片数量。一个分组应展开显示其中的单张照片。

2

AI Asks, You Answer

AI 提问，你回答

What you do: Open any photo group. PostMem shows you the photos and asks specific questions based on what AI identified. You answer by typing a few sentences or recording a voice note — 30 seconds is usually enough.

你做什么：打开任意一组照片。PostMem 展示照片并根据 AI 识别的内容提出具体问题。你通过打几句话或录语音来回答——30 秒通常就够了。

What AI does: Reads the visual content, identifies what it can determine (date, number of people, setting) and what it cannot (names, relationships, what happened). After you answer, AI organizes your responses into a written narrative attached to the photos.

AI 做什么：读取视觉内容，确定它能判断的（日期、人数、场景）和不能判断的（名字、关系、发生了什么）。你回答后，AI 把你的回答整理成与照片关联的书面叙述。

Realistic example: PostMem shows you 6 photos from a holiday dinner. It identifies 8 people, a turkey, and wood paneling. It asks: "Who are the people at this table?" and "What was the occasion?" You type: "That's my aunt Carol and uncle Pete at the head of the table. This was Thanksgiving 1998, the year Pete brought his accordion and played after dinner. Mom almost killed him." That becomes the opening of the story.

真实示例：PostMem 向你展示一次节日晚餐的 6 张照片。它识别出 8 个人、一只火鸡和木质墙板。它问："桌旁的人是谁？""这是什么场合？"你输入："坐在主位的是我 Carol 阿姨和 Pete 叔叔。这是 1998 年感恩节，Pete 那年带了手风琴晚餐后演奏。妈妈差点把他杀了。"这成为故事的开头。

A quieter example: PostMem groups 8 photos of the same woman in a garden across 7 years. It asks: "Who is this person?" and "Is this the same location?" You record a voice note: "That's my mom. That's her garden on Elm Street. She spent every Saturday morning out there. The tomatoes were her pride and joy." Fifteen seconds of talking. A story that would never have been captured otherwise.

一个更安静的例子：PostMem 将同一位女性在花园里跨越 7 年的 8 张照片归为一组。它问："这个人是谁？""这是同一个地方吗？"你录了一段语音："那是我妈妈。那是她在 Elm 街的花园。她每个周六早上都在那里。西红柿是她的骄傲。"十五秒的讲述。一个否则永远不会被记录的故事。

产品截图：单个照片组的问答界面——展示一排 3-4 张照片，下方是 AI 生成的问题，以及文字输入/语音按钮。最好展示一个已回答和一个待回答的问题。

3

Share and Preserve

分享与保存

What you do: Share each story with a single link — via text message, email, or family group chat. Recipients don't need an account or app. When you've built up enough stories, compile them into a printed photo book.

你做什么：通过链接分享每个故事——发短信、邮件或家庭群聊。接收者不需要账号或 App。积累足够多的故事后，可以汇编成一本印刷照片书。

  • ✓ A shareable link for each story (no login required for viewers)
  • 每个故事一个分享链接（查看者无需登录）
  • ✓ A growing family archive organized by people, places, and time
  • 按人物、地点和时间组织的持续增长的家庭档案
  • ✓ Full export of all photos, stories, and metadata at any time
  • 随时导出所有照片、故事和元数据
  • ✓ Printed book compilation (coming soon)
  • 印刷书汇编（即将推出）

Realistic example: You share the Thanksgiving 1998 story link in your family group chat. Your cousin replies: "I forgot about the accordion! I have more photos from that night." She uploads 4 more photos. The story gets richer.

真实示例：你在家庭群聊中分享了 1998 年感恩节故事的链接。你表妹回复："我忘了手风琴的事！我还有那晚的更多照片。"她又上传了 4 张照片。故事变得更丰富了。

产品截图：一个完成的可分享故事页面——展示叙述文字与照片交替排列，一个"分享"按钮，以及带日期和人物标签的故事标题。

What PostMem Does NOT Do

PostMem 不做什么

We'd rather be honest now than disappoint you later:

我们宁愿现在坦诚，也不愿之后让你失望：

✗Does not invent facts. If you don't provide information, AI doesn't guess.
不会编造事实。如果你不提供信息，AI 不会猜测。
✗Does not produce stories without your input. AI organizes; you provide the memories.
没有你的输入不会产出故事。AI 整理；你提供记忆。
✗Does not work well with very few photos. If you have fewer than 20 photos, the grouping will be limited. Works best with 100+ photos.
照片很少时效果不好。如果少于 20 张照片，分组功能会受限。100 张以上效果最好。
✗Does not replace a professional oral historian. Designed for everyday family memory preservation, not academic-grade oral history.
不能替代专业口述历史学家。为日常家庭记忆保存而设计，不是学术级口述历史。
✗Cannot identify people it has never seen. AI detects faces, but you tell it who's who. After you identify someone once, AI recognizes them across your library.
不能识别从未见过的人。AI 检测到人脸，但由你告诉它是谁。你识别一次后，AI 在整个照片库中都能认出。

Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

Do I need to be a good writer?▾

我需要擅长写作吗？

No. You don't write anything — you answer questions. PostMem shows you photos and asks things like "Who is this?" and "What happened here?" You answer with a few typed words or a 30-second voice note. AI organizes your answers into a readable narrative.

不需要。你不需要写任何东西——你回答问题。PostMem 展示照片并提问"这是谁？""这里发生了什么？"你用几个字或一段 30 秒的语音回答。AI 把你的回答整理成可读的叙述。

How is this different from Google Photos?▾

这和 Google Photos 有什么不同？

Google Photos and iCloud store and sort your photos. They'll group by face or location, but they don't capture the stories behind them. PostMem takes organized photos and turns them into written stories with names, context, and meaning.

Google Photos 和 iCloud 存储整理你的照片。它们能按人脸或地点分组，但不会捕捉背后的故事。PostMem 把整理好的照片变成有名字、有背景、有意义的文字故事。

How long does it take to get my first story?▾

多久能得到第一个故事？

About 10-15 minutes from upload to finished story. Upload photos (2-4 min), review AI-grouped clusters (1-2 min), answer questions about one group (3-5 min), review and edit (2-3 min). Subsequent stories are faster as faces are already identified.

从上传到完成故事大约 10-15 分钟。上传照片（2-4 分钟），查看 AI 分组（1-2 分钟），回答一组的问题（3-5 分钟），审核编辑（2-3 分钟）。后续故事更快，因为人脸已被识别。

Can multiple family members contribute?▾

多个家人可以参与吗？

This feature is coming soon. Right now, one person manages the account. We're building family collaboration so multiple people can add their own memories to the same photo groups — because different people remember different details.

这个功能即将推出。目前由一个人管理账户。我们正在构建家庭协作功能，让多人能向同一组照片添加自己的记忆——因为不同的人记得不同的细节。

What happens to my data if PostMem shuts down?▾

如果 PostMem 关闭了我的数据会怎样？

You can export your full archive (photos, stories, metadata) at any time in standard formats. We're also building automated backup notifications. Your stories belong to you, not to us.

你可以随时以标准格式导出完整档案（照片、故事、元数据）。我们还在构建自动备份通知。你的故事属于你，不是我们的。

Is my data used to train AI?▾

我的数据会被用来训练 AI 吗？

No. Your photos and stories are never used to train any AI model. They are stored encrypted, accessible only to you, and deletable at any time.

不会。你的照片和故事永远不会被用于训练任何 AI 模型。它们加密存储，只有你能访问，并且随时可以删除。
